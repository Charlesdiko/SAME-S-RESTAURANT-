About SAME'S RESTAURANT

We serve authentic Cebu delicacies like Utan Bisaya, Bolabola, Lumpia, Adobo, and Pancit. Every dish is prepared with love, preserving the tradition and flavors of Cebuano cuisine.

Meet the Owner

Lina Romero — born and raised in Boljoon, Cebu. From a young age she learned traditional Cebuano cooking from her grandmother. Her passion for local flavors and hospitality inspired her to open SAME'S RESTAURANT in 1995. Over the years she has built a warm, welcoming place where every guest feels like family.

About Her

  • Passionate about Cebuano cuisine & culture
  • Family-oriented and customer-focused
  • Committed to using fresh local ingredients
  • Friendly and welcoming vibe to every visitor

History

SAME'S RESTAURANT was established in 1995 by Lina Romero at 242 Nabor Derama St., Boljoon, Cebu. Lina blended her family’s original recipes with modern touches, allowing the restaurant to grow and attract more visitors. Today, it remains a proud Cebuano landmark.

The Success

Started as a small family food stall, the restaurant grew through consistency, care, and patience. Today, it serves locals, travelers, and students daily, standing as a recognized local food spot.

The Challenges

The owner faced limited tools, uncertain direction, and slow growth. Patience, steady improvement, and word-of-mouth helped the restaurant become a trusted local spot.

10 Years Later

After ten years, Same’s Restaurant remains warm and familiar. Early struggles became memories that shaped its growth. It grows through trust, consistency, and care, proving patience and small beginnings lead to meaningful success.

Government Support

Through proper registration, permits, seminars, and local initiatives, the Philippine government helped the business grow. Recognition and guidance gave the owner confidence to continue despite challenges.

A Message from the Owner

To everyone who walked through our doors, thank you for trusting us. Same’s Restaurant started with uncertainty, but your support gave it life. Every meal you shared here helped us grow, learn, and continue.

This place exists because of you. We promise to keep serving food made with care, honesty, and gratitude. Maayong Salamat!

— Lina Romero
